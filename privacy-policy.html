Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Girlfriend Gatekeeper ("the extension") is a Chrome browser extension that helps users limit time spent on social media websites by displaying a full-screen reminder when a configurable time limit is reached.

Data We Collect

The extension stores the following data exclusively on your local device using Chrome's chrome.storage.local and chrome.storage.session APIs:

  • Photos, GIF images, or video files you upload as your reminder media
  • Audio files you upload as background music
  • Your configured settings (time limits, break duration, caller name, reminder message, tracked sites list)
  • Per-site time usage counters (reset daily)
  • Active break state and warning flags (session-only, cleared when browser closes)

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No data is ever transmitted to any server or third party
  • No analytics, telemetry, or usage tracking of any kind
  • No personal information is collected, stored remotely, or shared
  • The extension makes zero network requests
  • No third-party SDKs, tracking libraries, or advertising are included

Permissions Used

  • storage — Persist your settings and usage counters locally on your device
  • alarms — Fire a 1-minute periodic timer to accumulate usage time while the service worker may be sleeping
  • tabs — Query open tabs to find user-configured social media sites and send the time-limit overlay to all affected tabs simultaneously
  • activeTab — Show a test overlay on the currently active tab when you click the test button in the popup
  • scripting — Re-inject the content script into tabs after extension updates when the original script has been orphaned
  • unlimitedStorage — Store user-uploaded photos and videos (as base64 data URLs, potentially 10–100 MB) without hitting Chrome's default 10 MB quota
  • notifications — Show a system notification 5 minutes before you reach your time limit, and when a break period ends
  • host_permissions (<all_urls>) — Users can add any website to their tracked list; the extension must be able to inject reminders on user-defined domains

Data Deletion

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by:

  • Clicking 重置 (Reset) in the extension popup to clear usage statistics
  • Clicking the clear (✕) buttons to remove uploaded media
  • Uninstalling the extension, which removes all associated local storage

Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the "Last updated" date above will be changed. Continued use of the extension constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
